Photographer Based in Ottawa, Ontario.

Mikaela is a Canadian photographer and storyteller based in Ottawa, Ontario. She graduated from Concordia University's Mel Hoppenheim School of Cinema with a BFA in Film Production and is currently studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. In her work, Mikaela seeks to highlight unique Canadian identities and tell visual stories through digital media.
