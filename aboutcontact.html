About Mikaela.

Hello! My name is Mikaela, and I'm a Canadian photographer and digital storyteller. I graduated with a BFA in Film Production from Concordia University, and I'm currently completing an Ontario College Diploma in Interactive Media Design from Algonquin College in Ottawa, Ontario.

I have always been a storyteller, interested in finding new and innovative ways to tell stories through digital media. My goal is to use photography to share Canada's unique natural and cultural identity.

I've had the opportunity to travel to some of our most beautiful provinces, sharing our uniquely beautiful landscapes through photography and videography. Some of my favourite photographs of Canadian landscapes have been featured and shared by CBC and Canadian Geographic, organizations showcasing Canadian natural and cultural heritage that I look up to. Some of these photographs can be found here and here!
